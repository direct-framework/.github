# Roadmap

Roadmap as envisaged at the start of 2026.

## Short-term (0–12 months)
- [ ] Focus: consolidation and usability*
- [ ] Publish a DOI-backed framework report (version 1).
- [ ] Publish this vision and roadmap document with a DOI.
- [ ] Refine the competency structure and skill scales.
- [ ] Redesign the framework spreadsheet for clarity and reuse.
- [ ] Integrate existing training catalogues compiled by relevant projects and initiatives.
- [ ] Develop an initial set of reference role profiles.
- [ ] Improve the usability and accessibility of the web application.

## Medium-term (1–2 years)
*Focus: adoption and embedding*

- [ ] Expand training mappings and role profiles.
- [ ] Support pilot adoption within institutions and projects.
- [ ] Develop support for institutional and team-level use.
- [ ] Strengthen governance and contribution processes.
- [ ] Secure sustainable funding for core maintenance and development.
- [ ] Establish formal links with aligned national and international initiatives.

## Long-term (3–5 years)
*Focus: sustainability and recognition*

- [ ] Embed DIRECT within institutional workforce planning.
- [ ] Achieve formal recognition or endorsement from relevant professional bodies.
- [ ] Support longitudinal use for career development and evaluation.
- [ ] Ensure long-term maintenance through stable governance and funding.
- [ ] Enable international adoption and adaptation.
